About us

Quizzers' Club is the official quizzing society of Manit which has been in existence since February 2013. QCM is a society formed in Manit by a bunch of quizzing prodigies of Manit, intended to promote the quizzing, group discussion, debate and general knowledge among the students of various colleges.Quizzers' Club has conducted various successful events.

Quizzers' Club Manit is an official quizzing society of Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology Bhopal. Quizzers' Club Manit was started in Feb 2013. We are the group of enthusiastic quizzers, executives and designers. The purpose of this quizzing club is to increase the level of general awareness amongst the student community. Apart from this we also organize many events throughout the year in which students from different colleges of India participate.

The main events organized by Quizzers' Club Manit are Vihaan, TQQW, Quizzotica and Manthan. We try to bring like-minded quizzing enthusiasts together on a platform for mutual sharing of ideas and probing the globe of knowledge. Quizzers' Club Manit provides opportunity to the students to show their quizzing skills. We are planning to conduct more quizzes online due to this pandemic and we are also working with other colleges to conduct quizzes with them as well. We are responsible for conducting quizzes in our college. We are also active in LinkedIn. We posts question on a daily basis on our instagram handle.

Our Events

Vihaan

QCM's session starts with VIHAAN. VIHAAN is one of the first events for the first years ( even before the freshers evening) and they get to know what awaits them in the future. VIHAAN is a platform for students, amateurs quizzers and newcomers to prove their mettle in quizzing.
VISION - QCM’s vision with VIHAAN is to make quizzing more popular among freshers.

Prizes
Cash Prizes worth Rs. 500/- and more
Eligibility
Only for Freshers of MANIT

Quizzotica

QUIZZOTICA is QCM’s attempt to make the largest Technical fest in central India “TECHNOSEARCH” more fun and exciting. Quizzotica has 2 rounds , with the first round being full of fun activities and competitions to be performed in groups which tests your creativity and knowledge. The second round consists of an exciting quiz with different sub-rounds which make the event more challenging.
VISION- To make quizzing more fun and exciting.

Prizes
Cash Prizes worth Rs. 500/- and more
Eligibility
Open for all

Manthan

Manthan is QCM’s flagship event. Manthan’s first-round consist of quizzes in different colleges across the country. In 2018, our team visited more than 15 colleges across Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan to make MANTHAN 2018 our most successful event till date. The second round is a formal quiz organized by the best quizmasters of the state. Each student represents their college which leads to a better competition and bragging rights.
VISION - To find the best quizzer of the country and to make quizzing more mainstream in other colleges.

Prizes
Prizes worth Rs. 10,000/- and more
Eligibility
Open for all(All over India)

TQQW

The Quarantine Quizzing Week (TQQW) - Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, Manthan’20 got cancelled. But the team tried thier best to replace Manthan with our longest and first online event till date. TQQW was our first online event held over a week, with different topics for each of the seven days. TQQW received an overall positive response and we are motivated to organize more such online events. In TQQW we organized quiz on the daily basis for a week on topics like history, business,sports etc. The winners were awarded with cash prize and certificates, and the event got huge overwhelming response. Although, we wish this quarantine never hit you again but more events like this will surely spice up your knowledge.
VISION - To encourage online quizzing and increase interaction during the lockdown.

Prizes
Cash Prizes worth Rs. 1000/- and more
Eligibility
Open for all
